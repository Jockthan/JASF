My Journey into Missionary Work and the Birth of Just a Smile Foundation

Growing up, I always loved spending time with kids. Since my church regularly sends out members for missionary work, I eagerly volunteered whenever the opportunity arose. However, despite my enthusiasm, I was never selected.

That changed when a family member from India came to visit us for ministry. One morning, as we sat together for breakfast, they shared how they had been praying for an opportunity to do mission work in Kenya. At that moment, I knew this was my chance. I turned to my parents and said, "This is my time to go." Without hesitation, they supported me, and soon, my tickets and visa were booked.

When I arrived in the beautiful country of Kenya, I was blessed with the opportunity to visit different schools. Out of my deep passion for children and faith, I taught Bible lessons, shared Bible stories, and helped the kids memorize verses. Their joy was infectious—they were always cheerful despite the difficult conditions they lived in. The roads leading to their schools and churches were often muddy and unpaved. It broke my heart to see many children wearing tattered clothes—torn socks, worn-out bags, and thin sweaters. Some classrooms relied on sunlight because they lacked proper lighting, and many children had to bring their younger siblings along because their parents were unable to care for them.

As I roamed the streets of Nairobi, I saw children sniffing drugs and engaging in activities that no child should ever experience. It shattered me to realize that no one was there to care for them. That moment sparked a thought in my heart: "Why not build a home for these children? A place where they can receive love, care, and the respect they deserve."

With that vision, I founded Just a Smile Foundation, headquartered in West Africa, Nigeria. Our goal is to reach not only children in Africa but also humanity as a whole. My team and I are committed to making a positive change in today’s chaotic world, starting with the lives of these precious children.

With your support, this dream can become a reality. Let's put a smile on a child's face today and create a lasting impact in our world.

Yours sincerely,
Marvin Jasmine Babu
CEO, Just a Smile Foundation
